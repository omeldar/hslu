Information

On this website you can check the weather at any location on the world. It will give you information about temperature and the weather conditions of this location. It has been created by Eldar Omerovic as semester project for the WEBT (Web-Tech) class at the University of Applied Sciences and Arts. It features cookies to prevent loss of the last searched result. By choosing to use this application you accept the necessary cookies. Dont worry, if you didnt search any for any weahter information so far, this site didn't store anything yet. It creates cookies as soon as you search for weather information.

How to use it

1. Choose the location by entering the name of the city and the country the city is located in.

2. Choose the date you want the weather information from.

3. Choose wether you want your temperature results in Fahrenheit or Celsius.
